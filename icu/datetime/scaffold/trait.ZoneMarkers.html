Expand description

A trait associating types for time zone formatting (input types and data markers).

This is a sealed trait implemented on field set markers.

๐ง This trait is considered unstable; it may change at any time, in breaking or non-breaking ways, including in SemVer minor releases. Do not implement this trait in userland unless you are prepared for things to occasionally break.
